The geometric point is an invisible thing. Therefore, it must be deffined as an incorporeal thing. Considered in terms of substance, it equals zero.
Hidden in this zero, however, are various attributes whiche are "human" in nature". We think of this zero in relation to the greatest possible brevity, i.e, to the highest degree of restraint which, nevertheless, speaks.
Thus we look upon the geometric point as the ultimate and most singular union of silence and speech
In the flow of speech, the point symbolizeds interruption, non-existence (negative element), and at the same time it forms a bridge from one existence to another (positive element). In this writing, this constitutes its inner significance

in the flow of any sentence used in every day life the point has a traditional sound,
